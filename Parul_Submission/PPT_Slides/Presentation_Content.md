# Presentation Content

## Slide 1 — Title
Bluestock Mutual Fund Capstone
Subtitle: Data Engineering, Analytics, and Dashboarding for Mutual Fund Insights

## Slide 2 — Project Objective
To build an end-to-end analytics workflow for mutual fund data covering ETL, storage, analysis, and dashboard preparation.

## Slide 3 — Data and Methodology
- Source datasets: fund master, NAV, SIP inflows, scheme performance, investor transactions
- Process: ingestion, cleaning, schema creation, database loading, visualization
- Tools: Python, SQLite, Power BI, notebooks

## Slide 4 — Key Insights
- Equity funds are prominent in the dataset
- NAV patterns vary by fund and category
- SIP activity shows strong investor participation signals
- The prepared dashboard enables interactive analysis

## Slide 5 — Dashboard Features
- Industry overview page
- Fund performance page
- Investor analytics page
- SIP and market trends page

## Slide 6 — Business Value
The project supports portfolio monitoring, investor segmentation, and evidence-based fund recommendations.

## Slide 7 — Conclusion
The Bluestock capstone demonstrates a complete analytics pipeline and a strong foundation for future enhancement and deployment.
